# 🎤 3-Minute Live Hackathon Demo Script

> Use this exact script and sequencing to maximize judge scores during the live demo or presentation video.

---

## ⏱️ Timeline overview
- **0:00 - 0:45**: Problem Statement & Cognitive Brain-Lock
- **0:45 - 1:30**: Syllabus Parser & AI Morning Briefing
- **1:30 - 2:15**: Emergency Rescue Mode, Scope Simplifier & Google Calendar Block allocation
- **2:15 - 2:45**: Draft Co-Pilot & Metric Analytics
- **2:45 - 3:00**: High-Impact Closing

---

## 📝 Script Walkthrough

### 🚨 [0:00 - 0:45] PART 1: The Problem & Brain Lock
*(Start on the main landing page, or open `/pitch` to Slide 1)*

**Presenter:**
> "Hi, I'm a computer science senior. It’s midnight, and my 20-page syllabus for a complex Machine Learning assignment was just released. 
> Looking at the long instructions, cognitive overload takes over. I freeze, procrastinate, and suddenly it's 8 hours before the deadline. 
> Panic sets in. 
> 
> Most task managers tell you *what* you are failing to do. We built **CRUNCH**, an active **AI Emergency Deadline Rescue Agent** that takes you by the hand and guides you through the crisis."

---

### 🔎 [0:45 - 1:30] PART 2: Syllabus Parsing & Morning Briefing
*(Navigate to `/pitch` Slide 2 & 3 or `/dashboard`)*

**Presenter:**
> "Let's upload that syllabus PDF. Immediately, CRUNCH’s **Gemini Vision Parser** extracts the exact grading rubrics and requirements that actually matter for a passing grade, bypassing the fluff.
> 
> When I log in each morning, I don't see a flat todo list. I am greeted by my **AI Tactical Morning Briefing**. 
> It tells me my exact priority, highlights the single biggest bottleneck risk, forecasts the focus hours needed, and gives me a single actionable button: **START WORKING**."

---

### ⚡ [1:30 - 2:15] PART 3: The Emergency Rescue Cockpit
*(Navigate to `/demo` or trigger Rescue Mode)*

**Presenter:**
> "When the deadline drops under 24 hours, or when we click 'I NEED RESCUE NOW', CRUNCH transforms into a high-alert **Rescue Cockpit**. 
> The UI switches to high-contrast dark mode, hiding all sidebars, distracting tabs, and external noise. 
> 
> A large countdown timer ticks in. 
> In this mode, we focus on **one single subtask at a time**, paired with custom 'If-Then' implementation intentions. 
> If stress peaks, we trigger the **Scope Simplifier**. Gemini instantly re-evaluates the syllabus, trimming non-critical styling or premium components to guarantee we meet the core passing criteria."

---

### ✍️ [2:15 - 2:45] PART 4: Draft Co-Pilot & Schedule Mapping
*(Show the Draft Assistant box and Calendar Blocks on `/demo`)*

**Presenter:**
> "Instead of staring at a blank text file, we compile our submission in the split-screen **Draft Co-Pilot**. With a single click, we can expand or shorten sections directly matching our rubric.
> 
> CRUNCH also synchronizes with **Google Calendar** to lock in focused blocks. If credentials aren't configured, it automatically fallbacks to **Suggested Schedule Mode** with zero-latency local caching, meaning it never crashes under pressure.
> 
> Our performance tracker keeps score of tasks completed, and drafts compiled, rewarding us with focus XP."

---

### 🏆 [2:45 - 3:00] PART 5: Impact & Closing
*(Open the `/pitch` Slide 8 with the Success Cup)*

**Presenter:**
> "CRUNCH doesn't just manage tasks; it rescues them. By turning cognitive overload into high-intensity structured sprints, we turn panic into passing grades. 
> 
> Thank you, and we are ready for your questions!"
